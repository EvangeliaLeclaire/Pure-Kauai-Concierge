Brand
Background
Pure Kauai was founded in 2001. With a long-standing stake in
the market, Pure Kauai has been successful in crafting and
maintaining a desirable product for it’s A-list clientele. Pure
Kauai offers the most exclusive collection of luxury homes on
Kauai's north shore, combined with the best, most carefully
selected and highest-level services and activities Kauai has to
offer. We offer an intimate human experience in a world lacking
human connection.
Pure Kauai invites people to experience the best of Kauai, while
keeping it Kauai.

B R A N D L O G O
Logo
The logo design of Pure Kauai reflects the brand's
commitment to elegance, luxury, and the natural beauty
of Kauai. With clean, minimalist lines the logo embodies
both the serene island atmosphere and the refined
experience guests can expect. The typography is sleek
and modern, evoking a sense of sophistication, while
subtle design elements—such as the incorporation of
natural shapes—capture the essence of Kauai’s tides
and heartbeat.
