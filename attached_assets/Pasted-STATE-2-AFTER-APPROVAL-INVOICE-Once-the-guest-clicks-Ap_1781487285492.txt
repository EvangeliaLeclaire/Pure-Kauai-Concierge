The tab label changes to Your Invoice

The document title shows: Pure Kauai Experience Invoice

Generate a sequential invoice number formatted as PK-2025-[4 digit number] based on the order itineraries were approved

Below the title show: Invoice Number: PK-2025-0001

Below that show: Invoice Date: [approval date]

Below that show: Prepared for: [Guest Name]

Below that show: Status: Confirmed in green color with a checkmark

Below that show: Deposit Due: [approval date plus 7 days]

Below that show: Balance Due: [check-in date minus 30 days]

Remove the Approve button entirely — replace with a green Confirmed badge

Show this text instead: Your journey has been confirmed. Your concierge will be in touch within 24 hours to process your deposit and confirm all reservations.

The download button says: Download Invoice PDF

The print button says: Print Invoice

The totals section shows: Invoice Total

The deposit line shows the actual amount due with the due date

ADD a signature line at the bottom of the invoice PDF only:

Guest Signature: _________________________ Date: _____________

Authorized by: _________________________ Date: _____________
UPDATE THE PDF ROUTES:

Before approval: GET /api/itineraries/:slug/quote-pdf downloads a Quote PDF

After approval: GET /api/itineraries/:slug/invoice-pdf downloads an Invoice PDF

Both use the same pdfkit formatting but with different titles, status, and content

The invoice PDF includes the invoice number, approval date, payment due dates, and signature lines

The quote PDF does not include invoice number or signature lines
UPDATE THE JOURNEY PDF:

The journey PDF is always available regardless of approval status

It never mentions pricing or payment

It is purely the experiential itinerary document
